Software
Careers in software development involve designing, coding, testing, and maintaining software applications. Professionals in this field create solutions for desktop, mobile, and web platforms, working as software engineers, web developers, mobile app developers, and game developers.
Networking
Networking professionals design, implement, and maintain computer networks that connect devices, enabling communication and data exchange. Career paths include network administrators, network engineers, cloud network specialists, and IT support technicians..
Data Management
Data management careers involve collecting, storing, and analyzing data to support decision-making. Professionals in this field work as database administrators, data analysts, data engineers, and data scientists, ensuring data integrity, security, and accessibility.
Cybersecurity
Cybersecurity careers focus on protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access and cyber threats. Roles include cybersecurity analysts, penetration testers, security engineers, and incident response specialists, who ensure the safety of digital information.

The Computer and Information Technology (CIT) program at Purdue University prepares students for careers in technology management, cybersecurity, networking, and software development.

Purdue CIT offers a variety of degree options, including Bachelor's degrees in Network Engineering Technology, Systems Analysis and Design, Cybersecurity, and more.

CIT graduates pursue careers in software development, network administration, cybersecurity analysis, IT consulting, and more.
